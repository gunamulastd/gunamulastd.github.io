How a project runs

Four stages, clear deliverables at each one. No surprises.

  1. Discover

    We learn your business, your customers and your goals, and agree what success looks like.

  2. Design

    Wireframes first, then full visual design. You review and approve every screen before we build.

  3. Build

    We code the approved design, test it on real devices, and tune it for speed and accessibility.

  4. Launch

    We deploy, connect your domain and analytics, and hand over with training and documentation.
